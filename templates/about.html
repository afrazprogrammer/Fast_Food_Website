Our Founder

Giovanni "Gio" Lombardi, hailing from Naples, Italy, is the visionary behind "Lombardi's Express," a 
fast food restaurant known for blending traditional Italian flavors with modern convenience. Gio's culinary
journey began in his family's kitchen and led him to graduate top of his class from the Culinary Institute of
Naples. He earned several prestigious accolades, including Best Young Chef of Italy (2005), a Gold Medal at
the International Culinary Expo (2007), and Chef of the Year (2010). In 2012, Gio moved to the U.S. to
revolutionize fast food. Lombardi's Express quickly gained popularity due to its innovative menu,
exceptional customer service, and sustainability initiatives. The restaurant has since expanded nationwide, and Gio 
also established the Lombardi Culinary Scholarship Fund to support aspiring chefs. Gio's legacy continues to inspire
and set new standards in the culinary world.

History of Quick Taste! 
Founded in 1980 by Giovanni "Gio" Lombardi, Quick Taste evolved from a single roadside stand in Austin, 
Texas, to a national chain with over 500 locations. Known for its high-quality meals and innovative
"Quick-Grill" system introduced in 1985, Quick Taste set new industry standards for fast service and quality.
The company emphasizes community, sustainability, and continuous improvement, sourcing local ingredients and
supporting charitable initiatives like the "Taste of Hope" program. Quick Taste is celebrated not just for its
delicious food and speedy service, but also for its positive societal impact and inclusive workplace culture.

Our Team at Quick Taste!

Chef Lucas Ramirez

Chef Ramirez is renowned for his dynamic energy and innovative spirit in the culinary world. After graduating from the prestigious Culinary Institute of America, Lucas honed his skills in various high-end restaurants across New York City. He gained widespread recognition for his modern take on traditional Mexican cuisine, combining bold flavors with artistic presentation. Lucas was awarded the "Rising Star Chef" title by Food & Wine magazine and won the "Innovative Chef of the Year" award at the Culinary Excellence Awards. His restaurant, "Sabor Nuevo," was also featured in the Michelin Guide.

Chef Emily Nguyen

Chef Nguyen is known for her passion for plant-based cuisine and her creative approach to vegan and vegetarian dishes. Emily graduated top of her class from Le Cordon Bleu in Paris and quickly made a name for herself with her unique and flavorful plant-based creations. She worked as a sous-chef at the renowned "Green Table" in San Francisco before opening her own vegan bistro. Emily received the "Best New Chef" award from VegNews and was a finalist in the "Young Chef of the Year" competition. Her restaurant, "Green Harmony," was named one of the "Top 10 Vegan Restaurants" by Eater.

Chef Giovanni Russo

Chef Russo brings decades of experience and a deep appreciation for traditional Italian cuisine. Giovanni started his culinary journey in his family's trattoria in Tuscany and later moved to Rome, where he became the executive chef at a top-rated restaurant. His dedication to preserving authentic Italian flavors while incorporating modern techniques has earned him widespread acclaim. Giovanni has been awarded two Michelin stars and received the "Lifetime Achievement Award" from the Italian Culinary Federation. He has also published several best-selling cookbooks on Italian cuisine.
